[h1]Men-At-Arms Manager[/h1]

[b]Efficiently manage and reorganize your Men-At-Arms with an intuitive interface.[/b]

[h2]Overview[/h2]
The Men-At-Arms Manager adds a powerful new interface to your military screen, allowing you to redistribute soldiers and regiments between your Men-At-Arms without the tedious process of disbanding and recreating units.

[h2]Key Features[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Transfer Soldiers[/b] - Move individual soldiers between regiments of the same type
[*] [b]Transfer Regiments[/b] - Instantly move entire regiment stacks between armies
[*] [b]Seamless Integration[/b] - Adds a single button to your existing military interface (F3)
[*] [b]Multi-Language Support[/b] - Full localization in English, French, German, Spanish, Polish, Russian, Chinese, and Korean
[*] [b]Upcoming-Feature[/b] - Retrain your Men-At-Arms to different unit types (coming soon)
[/list]

[h2]How It Works[/h2]
[olist]
[*] Open your Military screen (F3)
[*] Click the [b]"Men-At-Arms Manager"[/b] button
[*] Select source and target regiments
[*] Transfer soldiers or entire regiments between them
[*] Confirm your changes or adjust as needed
[/olist]

[h2]Requirements[/h2]
[list]
[*] Men-At-Arms must be lowered (not raised/deployed)
[*] Cannot be used during wartime
[/list]

[h2]Compatibility[/h2]
This mod only adds new features and does not modify existing game files. It should be compatible with most other mods unless they heavily modify the military interface.

[h2]Planned Features[/h2]
[list]
[*] Retrain regiments to different unit types
[/list]
